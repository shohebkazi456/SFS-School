ST. FRANCIS DE SALES HIGH SCHOOL

Tirawade, Kadgaon(P.O), Gargoti (Via), Bhudargad (Tk.) Kolhapur (Dt) M S - 416 210, Landline: (02324) 233632

St. Francis De Sales High school is a Christian Minority private Institution recognized by the Ministry of education Govt. Of Maharashtra and managed by the Missionaries Of St. Francis De sales.(MSFS)

This minority school although established by Fransalian Education Society primarily for Catholics, desires to extend its services to all people, irrespective of caste, creed or race (Article 30, Constitution of India)

THE MISSIONARIES OF ST. FRANCIS DE SALES This is the society of Fathers and Brothers of (of the Catholic Church) founded by Fr. Peter Mermier in 1836 at Annecy, France. His aim was to instruct the youth and lead them along the right path by using techniques like preaching missions, conducting camps and running educational institutions. The missionaries of St. Francis De sales are spread today all over the world and they are involved in activities like preaching, social service, running schools, technical institutes and boardings for the well - being of the people.

Though education, the Fransalians aim to make the Students responsible and worthy citizens of the country and to bring them to the knowledge of Christ. If they lay claim to any other purpose, it must lie in the boldness of vision, Characteristic of Religious who have dedicated themselves totally for the sake of others, in a study of the trends of society and in the effective guidance of the students, preparing them for the future. This is an extremley difficult task and the Fransalians appeal to parents and guardians to co - oprate in this worthy and arduous and responsible task.
